Introduction

At the Sociolegal Computing Lab, our interdisciplinary research leverages natural language processing to critically assess the impacts of AI on legal and societal frameworks.

In pursuit of AI that aligns with diverse cultural values, we scrutinize these systems through the lens of non-Western legal traditions.

The Lab curates unique resources, such as Traditional Chinese corpora, facilitating data-driven inquiries into the intricate connections among technology, culture, and law.

Our goal is to steer AI development toward serving the broader social good.
